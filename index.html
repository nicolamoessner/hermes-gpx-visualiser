Hermes GPX Visualiser Information
Hermes GPX Visualiser (HGV) is a web application designed to provide users the ability to upload their personal GPX files and generate interactive maps plotting their route, in addition to displaying information such as the time taken, and the elevation across their journey.
If the users' GPX files contain additional data such as a recording of the heart rate or cadence, HGV also generates graphs to provide the user a visual perspective on how these data points vary over their journey.
  • There is a 10 file limit.
  • Only .gpx files are currently accepted.
